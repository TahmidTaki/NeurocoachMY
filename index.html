Welcome to Neurocoach Digital Lab

Neurocoach Digital Lab (NDL) is a center for brain measurements and treatments. All our services are fully dedicated to the well-being of children and adults with brain-related and behavioral problems.

At NDL, we combine knowledge and experience from the fields of psychology, neurology and computer science to make sure that everybody receives the best treatment possible. We measure the brain and behavior using state-of-the-art equipment and tests. And we use the same techniques in combination with our multidisciplinary experience to train the brain and mind in order to improve problems.

These are our services

Our team is specialised in the treatment of neurological diseases (e.g. stroke) and mental diseases (ADHD, depression, anxiety). We treat adults and children.

The Strokecoach program for stroke survivors offers a unique combination: daily training at home or in the center - always accompanied by your personal coach. In addition, we track your health status as well as your progress (monitoring). With us, you stay motivated and always train just right!

Learn More..

Neurofeedback is a brain training to improve concentration. The scientifically based treatment method is used in the treatment of concentration disorders, neurological as well as mental illnesses (including stroke, depression). It is suitable for children and adults.

Take a look into our NFB flyer

We make concentration performance measurable. This allows us to objectify impairments in everyday life.
